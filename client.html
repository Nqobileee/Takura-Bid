About Our Company

Zimbabwe Logistics Co. is a leading freight and logistics company based in Harare, specializing in reliable cargo transportation across Southern Africa. With over 3 years of experience in the industry, we have built a reputation for excellence in handling diverse cargo types including agricultural products, construction materials, and high-value shipments.

Our commitment to on-time delivery, professional service, and competitive rates has made us a trusted partner for businesses throughout Zimbabwe and the SADC region. We work with certified drivers and maintain a fleet of well-maintained vehicles to ensure your cargo reaches its destination safely and efficiently.

We pride ourselves on transparent communication, fair pricing, and building long-term relationships with both shippers and drivers. Our platform connects businesses with reliable transportation solutions while providing drivers with consistent, well-paying opportunities.
